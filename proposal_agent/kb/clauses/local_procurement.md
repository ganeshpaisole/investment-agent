## Local Procurement Clause

The proposal is subject to the customer's local procurement policies. Any supplier qualification, local content requirements, or pre-qualification documentation requested by the Customer will be provided in accordance with the stated timelines. Pricing and delivery commitments are conditional on procurement approvals.
