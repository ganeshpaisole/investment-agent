### Module-level SOW
- Finance: General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Asset Accounting — deliverables: configuration, chart of accounts redesign, reconciliation scripts, validations, UAT scenarios.
- MM (Materials Management): Inventory configuration, goods movement processes, valuation methods, integration points with warehouse and MES.
- SD (Sales & Distribution): Order-to-cash processes, pricing, billing, EDI/API integrations to logistics.
- PP (Production Planning): Master data alignment, planning run templates, integration to MES for production orders.
- BW/BI: Data extraction, reporting layers, executive dashboards and KPIs.

### Data Migration
