UPDATION OF PAN, KYC DETAILS AND NOMINATION

+ How can I register/update my Permanent Account Number (PAN) and KYC details?

Investors holding securities in physical form are requested to register/update their

KYC details including PAN, postal address, email address, bank details, etc. by submitting
duly filled and signed Form ISR-1 to the Registrar & Transfer Agent (R & T Agent) of the
Bank.

Investors holding securities in dematerialised form are requested to register/update their
KYC details with their respective Depository Participants.
