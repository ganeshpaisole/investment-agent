(oe Step by Step Process for Loading/Reloading FTC through YONO LITE Application: -
1. After Log in to Yono Lite > Click Manage Cards.
2. Under Manage Cards, click Prepaid Card.
a) Under Prepaid Card, Click View My Linked Prepaid Cards option to check whether your FTC is linked.

b) If not linked, please use the option Link My Prepaid Card to link the card.
3. For Loading, please click following option:
a) State Bank Foreign Travel Card > Reload My FTC Accept Terms and Conditions > Select the
Debit Account using drop down to proceed.

b) Using Drop down Select the Card to Load/Reload and select the currency, enter the amount and select
Purpose of Travel using drop down.

c) Enter all the details like, passport validity, VISA details, flight details.

4. After entering all details and accepting terms and conditions, click Submit button. On entering OTP transaction will
be sent for processing.

5. Under History Tab transaction status can be viewed.

6. Transactions initiated before 03:00 pm will be authorized on the same day and transaction initiated after 03:00 pm
and non-working day will be authorized on the next working day. Please keep sufficient balance till the transaction is
authorized.
