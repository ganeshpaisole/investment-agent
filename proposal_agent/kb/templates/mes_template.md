## Detailed SOW

### Module-level SOW
- Requirements & Process Design: Workshops with production, quality, and maintenance teams; define use-cases for OEE, traceability, and genealogy.
- Integration: PLC/SCADA interface design, data mapping, error handling, and reconciliation to ERP.
- Execution: MES business rules, work order execution, operator screens, and data validation.
- Reporting & Dashboards: OEE, quality trends, lot genealogy, and historian retention policies.

### Pilot & Rollout
- Pilot on single production line with acceptance criteria, then phased rollout across lines or sites.

### Support
- Post-deployment support, knowledge transfer, and runbook handover.
