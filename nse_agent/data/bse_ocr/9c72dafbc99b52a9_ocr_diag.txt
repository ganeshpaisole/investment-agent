Caution For Investors - RRP Semiconductors Limited

504346 RRP Semiconductors Limited

Investors’ attention is invited on the caution issued by Exchange on Oct 20, 2025, regarding
unusual price movement of the security viz RRP Semiconductor Limited (formerly known as GD
Trading and Agencies Limited) and the clarification issued by the company denying any
association with legendary cricketer Sachin Tendulkar and land allotment from
Government of Maharashtra.

The company vide its letter dated Nov 03, 2025, has filed a police complaint regarding
circulation of the misinformation through social media whereby various stock
recommendation about the company has been circulated illegally and without any basis to mislead
investors.

The company vide its letter dated Nov 05, 2025, has announced that its Board of Directors
proposes to consider the withdrawal of the preferential allotment of equity shares previously
issued to promoters and non-promoters.

In view of the apparent misinformation in public domain and uncertainty attached to various
events associated with the company, investors are urged to exercise extreme care and caution

while transacting in the securities of the said company.

Issued in the interest of Investors.
